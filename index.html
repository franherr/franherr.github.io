Welcome!

I am a graduate student studying mathematics at University of Chicago. My research sits at the intersection of low-dimensional topology, geometry, and dynamics.

Math

I gravitate towards problems with hand-on examples, beautiful pictures, and an interplay between different levels of structure. Some reoccuring objects are branched coverings of surfaces, link complements, symmetry groups, and hyperbolic spaces.

Most recently, I have been thinking about depth one foliations of 3-manifolds and associated mapping classes on infinite-type surfaces.

Art

I love finding ways to communicate mathematics through the arts. I make dances, drawings, and videos about math. This interest takes on many different forms. My recent/current projects include...

  • a dance film about configuration spaces of points
  • making videos for my YouTube channel GrapefruitGecko
  • crocheting hyperbolic spaces and Seifert surfaces
  • thinking about patterns in modular multiplication tables.
